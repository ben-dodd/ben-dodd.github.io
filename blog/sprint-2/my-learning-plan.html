goals and strengths

long-term goal and career pathway

In many ways I would like to continue as I have been to this point (but with more success): finding projects I like and bringing my skills together to complete them. I would like to do more inventor-type projects involving Raspberry Pis and electronics.

Career-wise, I would love to work for a web or mobile app company. I know how much poorly-made apps can ruin your day, especially if you are made to use them for work. I think I have something special to offer - a unique set of skills. I would like to try both UX design and front-end developing.

strengths and limitations in learning

I like to test the limits of things while learning and play with the capabilities of different tools. I think I have a good attention span for exercises that many people find tedious. I like puzzles and things. Sorting lists. All that exciting stuff.

core skills i would like to develop at eda

I feel like I am pretty good at working in a team and getting along with others - but this could definitely be improved. I would like to become better at explaining my ideas and getting other people involved in making them happen.

commitment to the programme

how will i manage myself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives

Communication is key. I will make the effort to check in with others about where I'm at and offer help where I can. If I'm struggling, I'll make sure to contact the facilitators and ask for help.

what will i do when things are building up and starting to get to me

As above, I will communicate if I'm feeling overwhelmed. I understand that there is help I can access and will keep check of my mental health so I can manage the course along with other life commitments.

how and where i will seek help in a timely way

I will seek help from facilitators, and other students, when I need it. I will also need to explain to family and friends if I need help or if I need them to help me out with other commitments.

what do i expect from the facilitation team

I expect to have a reciprocal relationship of respect with the facilitation team. I know they'll treat my questions and concerns seriously, so I'm feeling confident I'll be able to get what I want out of this course.
